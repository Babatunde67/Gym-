High Intensity, Low Volume

This style of training focuses on lifting heavy weights with fewer total sets and reps. The goal is to maximise mechanical tension while keeping workouts short and focused, preventing muscle fatigue. By applying this training style in your lifts, your muscles still experience the same intensity needed for hypertrophy, while also allowing fresher, less fatigued, muscles for your other workouts.

Typical structure:

  • 2–3 sets per exercise
  • 4–6 reps per set
  • Longer rest periods (2–4 minutes)

Best used for big compound movements like squats, deadlifts, bench press, etc.
