Recovery

Recovery is just as important as your training. Without proper rest, your muscles cannot repair and grow, and your performance will plateau or decline.

Key recovery strategies include:

  • Sleep: Aim for 7-9 hours per night to optimize growth hormone release and cognitive function.
  • Nutrition: Eat sufficient protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats to replenish energy and repair muscle.
  • Active recovery: Light cardio, stretching, or mobility work helps improve blood flow and reduce soreness.

Other techniques such as foam rolling, massage, and contrast baths can accelerate recovery. Listen to your body and allow rest days when needed.
