Training Plans & Splits

Your training split is how you spread your workouts across the week. The goal is to hit each muscle group often enough to grow while still recovering properly.

Common splits:

  • Full Body: 3 days per week, all major muscles each session. Great for beginners or those with limited training days.
  • Upper / Lower: 4 days per week, alternating upper and lower body workouts. Ideal for intermediate lifters.
  • Push / Pull / Legs: 3â€“6 days per week, focused on movement patterns. Excellent for advanced lifters wanting higher frequency.

Choose a split that matches your schedule, recovery ability, and training goals. Consistency matters more than the exact template.
